What We Do

Every Document. Controlled. Traceable. Auditable.

IR-FZE designs and implements Document Control Systems that give your project a single source of truth for all technical documents — drawings, specifications, submittals, transmittals, and correspondence — fully auditable and authority-ready.

Whether you need to set up a system from scratch on a new UAE development or bring discipline to a live project that has lost control of its document flow, our team deploys a structured, practical DMS that your site team will actually use.

Our 4-Step Process

1
Audit & Requirements Capture
We review your current document landscape — existing filing, numbering conventions, transmittal practices, and authority submission requirements — to define the target system spec.
2
System Design & Procedures
We create your Document Control Procedure manual covering document numbering, revision control, transmittal protocols, approval workflows, and archive procedures aligned to UAE authority expectations.
3
DMS Configuration & Go-Live
We configure your chosen platform (Aconex, Procore, SharePoint, or custom) — setting up folder structures, permission levels, workflow automations, and document registers before going live.
4
Training & Ongoing Support
We train all document controllers and key users, provide quick-reference guides, and offer ongoing support during the critical first months of live operation.
