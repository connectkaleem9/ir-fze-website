What We Do

Control That Scales With Your Programme

IR-FZE designs and implements Project Management Systems tailored to the scale and complexity of your UAE programme — from single-project PMO setup to multi-contract programme controls across the Emirates.

We align your reporting structures, scheduling methodology, cost control frameworks, and risk processes to international best practice and UAE client expectations — giving leadership the live visibility they need to make decisions with confidence.

Our 4-Step Process

1
Current State Diagnostic
We assess your existing reporting, scheduling, and controls environment — identifying gaps, pain points, and the highest-value improvements available to your team.
2
Framework Design
We design a bespoke PMO framework covering governance structure, reporting cadence, scheduling methodology, risk register format, and KPI dashboards aligned to your client's requirements.
3
System Implementation
We implement the framework alongside your team — configuring tools, setting up templates, populating baseline schedules, and establishing live reporting workflows.
4
Handover & Capability Transfer
We train your project controls team and ensure sustainable operation — with optional ongoing advisory retainer for complex multi-year programmes.
