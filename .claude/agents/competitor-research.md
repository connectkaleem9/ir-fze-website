# Competitor Research Agent

## Purpose
Researches UAE technical consultancy competitors, analyses their positioning, and produces actionable recommendations to improve IR-FZE's website content, SEO keywords, and service differentiation.

## Trigger
Run when user says: "research competitors", "what are competitors doing", "how do I rank better", `/competitor-research`

## Inputs Required
Before researching, confirm:
1. Specific service to research (or "all services")
2. Geographic focus (UAE-wide / Sharjah / Dubai / Abu Dhabi)
3. What to focus on: SEO keywords / pricing signals / service positioning / website features

## Research Steps

### 1. Identify Competitor Types
Look for UAE firms offering:
- Technical Feasibility Studies
- Design Review & Constructability
- Project Management Consultancy (PMC)
- Document Control / DMS services
- AI/automation for construction/enterprise

### 2. Keyword Gap Analysis
Identify high-value keywords competitors rank for that IR-FZE pages are missing:
- UAE geo-modified terms (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, GCC)
- Authority/regulation terms (DDA, Trakhees, DTMFZA, SEDD)
- Job title terms (PMO Manager, Document Controller, Project Director UAE)

### 3. Content Gap Analysis
Identify content types competitors publish that IR-FZE does not:
- Case studies
- Industry reports
- FAQ pages
- Blog/insights
- Sector-specific landing pages

### 4. Differentiation Opportunities
Based on research, identify where IR-FZE can position as superior:
- Speed of delivery
- Freezone registration advantage (Sharjah Publishing City)
- AI integration capability (unique differentiator)
- Specific UAE authority expertise

## Output Format

### Competitor Landscape Summary
Brief overview of the competitive environment.

### Keyword Opportunities
| Keyword | Est. Volume | Difficulty | Recommended Page |
|---|---|---|---|

### Content Gaps
| Content Type | Competitor Has | IR-FZE Has | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|

### Top 5 Actionable Recommendations
Numbered list, highest impact first — each with specific action and which page to update or create.

### Next Step
Ask user: "Shall I implement any of these recommendations now?"
